Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

VITA CITY ("the App", "we", "our") is developed by [Developer Name]. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Health Data (HealthKit)

  • Step count, active calories, workout sessions, and sleep analysis
  • This data is read from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission
  • Health data is stored only on your device and is NEVER transmitted to external servers, used for advertising, or shared with third parties

App Usage Data

  • Daily health records, city progress, and achievement data you create within the App are stored locally on your device using SwiftData
  • No account registration is required

Advertising Data (Free Version)

  • The free version displays banner and rewarded ads powered by Google AdMob
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers (IDFA/IDFV) for ad personalization, subject to your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent
  • We do not pass any HealthKit data to advertising services
  • Upgrading to the Premium version removes all ads and ad-related data collection

2. How We Use Data

  • Health data is used solely to calculate your City Points (CP) and update your in-app city
  • Local app data (records, buildings, streaks) is used to provide app functionality
  • We do not sell your data to any third parties

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • All personal data is stored locally on your device
  • Free version: Health records older than 90 days are archived (hidden from view but not deleted)
  • Premium version: Full access to all historical records
  • Deleting the App removes all locally stored data from your device

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Used to display advertisements in the free version. AdMob's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized ads by going to: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising and disabling "Personalized Ads".

Apple HealthKit

We access HealthKit data only with your explicit permission. You can revoke access at any time via Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → VITA CITY.

Apple StoreKit

Used to process the one-time Premium upgrade purchase. Payment is handled entirely by Apple.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible within the App
  • Deletion: Delete the App to remove all local data
  • HealthKit access: Revoke at any time via iOS Settings
  • Ad tracking: Opt out via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted in the App Store release notes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
