Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Health data isn't showing up

Open iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → VITA CITY, and make sure all categories are allowed. Restart the app after granting access.

Q. My records disappeared

All data is stored on your iPhone only. Restore from an iOS backup, or re-enter recent logs. Enabling iCloud Backup helps prevent data loss.

Q. I purchased premium but it isn't active

Tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the Settings screen. This restores purchases tied to your Apple ID. Family Sharing is supported, so members of your Family Sharing group can use the same purchase.

Q. Step count isn't incrementing

Check iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Motion & Fitness and make sure motion tracking is enabled. Steps are not counted when you aren't carrying your iPhone.

Q. I can't build a building

Each building has a required cumulative CP. Check the "Required CP" in the City Management screen. Your map may also be full — map expansion unlocks automatically at 5,000 / 15,000 / 30,000 cumulative CP.

Q. Weather is always stormy

Today's CP below 100 triggers storms. Your streak and yesterday's CP raise a morning baseline, so consistent daily logging keeps the skies clearer.

Q. An izakaya or ruined building appeared

These are visual warnings that appear when you log 5+ drinks in a day. They fade naturally as you resume healthier logs.

Q. Is my data sent anywhere?

No. All health data stays on your iPhone. See our Privacy Policy for details.
