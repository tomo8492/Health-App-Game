VITA CITY turns your daily health habits into a thriving pixel-art city — the healthier you live, the bigger your city grows.

■ Earn Health Points (CP) across 5 axes
Log your daily habits in Exercise, Diet, Alcohol control, Sleep, and Lifestyle. Earn up to 500 CP per day and watch your city expand.

■ Build your dream city
Spend CP to construct buildings — gyms, stadiums, cafes, libraries and 28 types in total. Every structure reflects the healthy choices you make.

■ Seamless Apple Health integration
Steps, workouts, and sleep data are automatically pulled from HealthKit. Less manual entry, more motivation.

■ Streak bonuses & Achievements
Consecutive daily logging extends your streak and boosts city growth speed. Unlock 16 achievement badges as milestones.

■ WidgetKit support
See today's CP progress at a glance from your Home Screen or Lock Screen (small widget free; medium & large with Premium).

■ Premium — one-time purchase
Unlimited data history, detailed statistics charts, and medium/large widgets — all unlocked with a single purchase.
